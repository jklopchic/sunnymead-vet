Vaccinations

Protecting your pet's health through immunization

Vaccinations are key to maintaining your pet's health. Beginning with your first puppy or kitten visit, your doctor will talk to you about the vaccines she recommends for your pet. The Doctor will base her recommendations on your pet's lifestyle, health and other risk factors.

Dog Vaccinations

In general, we recommend that all dogs be vaccinated against:

  • Rabies
  • Distemper
  • Hepatitis
  • Parainfluenza
  • Parvo

At our hospital, this consists of two individual injections. Puppies require frequent boosters.

Cat Vaccinations

Cats should be vaccinated for rabies. Other vaccines like FIP and leukemia are recommended based on your cat's individual risk factors.
