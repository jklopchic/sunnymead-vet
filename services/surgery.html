Surgery

Full-service surgical care for your pet

We are a full service, surgical hospital offering both soft-tissue and orthopedic surgery at our location. All of surgeries from routine neuters to orthopedic procedures are monitored by a trained staff member whose sole job is to ensure that your pet has a safe, uneventful procedure.

In addition to conventional surgery, we also offer laser surgery and electrosurgery for specific cases.

Our Surgical Services Include:

  • Spay and neuter procedures
  • Soft tissue surgery
  • Orthopedic surgery
  • Tumor removal
  • Laser surgery
  • Electrosurgery
