Laser Therapy

Pain relief and accelerated healing

Therapeutic laser therapy uses light energy to stimulate cell regeneration and increase blood circulation. This non-invasive treatment can reduce pain and inflammation while speeding up the healing process.

Conditions That May Benefit from Laser Therapy:

  • Arthritis and joint pain
  • Post-surgical healing
  • Wound healing
  • Sprains and strains
  • Ear infections
  • Hot spots and skin conditions
  • Back pain

Laser therapy sessions are quick, painless, and most pets find them relaxing. Multiple treatments may be recommended for optimal results.
