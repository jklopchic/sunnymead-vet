Microchips

Permanent identification for your pet

A microchip is a tiny electronic chip, about the size of a grain of rice, that is implanted under your pet's skin. Each chip has a unique identification number that can be read by a scanner. If your pet is ever lost and brought to a shelter or veterinary hospital, they can scan for the microchip and contact you.

Why Microchip Your Pet?

  • Permanent identification that can't fall off or be removed
  • Greatly increases the chance of being reunited with a lost pet
  • Quick and relatively painless procedure
  • Lasts for the lifetime of your pet

We use HomeAgain microchips, which are registered in a national database. Don't forget to keep your contact information up to date!
