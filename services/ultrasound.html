Ultrasonography

Non-invasive imaging for soft tissue evaluation

Ultrasound is a non-invasive imaging technique that uses sound waves to visualize internal organs. It's particularly useful for examining soft tissues that don't show up well on X-rays.

Dr. Ontiveros has a special interest in ultrasonography and performs ultrasound exams at our hospital.

Common Uses for Ultrasound:

  • Pregnancy confirmation and monitoring
  • Abdominal organ evaluation (liver, spleen, kidneys, bladder)
  • Heart evaluation (echocardiography)
  • Tumor identification and staging
  • Guiding biopsies and aspirates
  • Evaluating fluid accumulation

Ultrasound is painless and most pets tolerate it very well without sedation.
