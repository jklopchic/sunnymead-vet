Radiology

Digital X-rays for detailed imaging

We use digital radiography (X-rays) to help diagnose a wide variety of conditions. Digital X-rays provide clearer images than traditional film and allow us to share images electronically with specialists when needed.

When X-rays May Be Needed:

  • Suspected fractures or bone injuries
  • Arthritis evaluation
  • Heart and lung problems
  • Abdominal issues
  • Foreign body ingestion
  • Dental problems
  • Cancer screening

Most X-rays can be taken with your pet awake, though sedation may be needed for some views or anxious patients.
