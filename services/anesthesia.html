Anesthesia

Safe, monitored anesthesia for all procedures

We understand that anesthesia can be a concern for pet owners. At Sunnymead Animal Hospital, we use the safest anesthesia protocols and continuously monitor your pet throughout any procedure.

Our Anesthesia Safety Measures:

  • Pre-anesthetic blood work to assess organ function
  • IV catheter for medication delivery and fluid support
  • Inhalant anesthesia for precise control
  • Continuous monitoring by trained staff
  • Heart rate and rhythm monitoring
  • Blood pressure monitoring
  • Oxygen saturation monitoring
  • Temperature regulation

A dedicated staff member monitors your pet from induction through recovery, ensuring the safest possible experience.
