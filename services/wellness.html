Wellness Examinations

Preventive care to keep your pet healthy

Regular Wellness Examinations are the key to maintaining your pet's health and to catch problems as early as possible. During your pet's wellness exam, the doctor will perform a full physical exam and discuss any findings with you.

Our comprehensive wellness exams include:

  • Complete physical examination
  • Weight and body condition assessment
  • Heart and lung evaluation
  • Dental health check
  • Skin and coat examination
  • Discussion of diet and nutrition
  • Parasite prevention recommendations
  • Vaccination updates as needed

We recommend annual wellness exams for adult pets and more frequent visits for puppies, kittens, and senior pets.
