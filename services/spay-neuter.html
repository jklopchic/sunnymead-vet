Spay and Neuter

Safe surgical sterilization for your pet

All spays and neuters include:

  • Presurgical pain medication
  • Presurgical antibiotic
  • IV-Catheter
  • Inhalational anesthesia
  • Monitoring by trained staff while under anesthesia
  • The surgical procedure
  • Pain Medication for home use

Other Additional Items

  • Pre-Anesthetic Lab work
  • Other concurrent procedures (dental, foxtail removal, etc)
  • E-Collar
