Pharmacy Services

Medications and preventatives for your pet

We maintain an in-house pharmacy stocked with medications and preventatives your pet may need. Our staff can fill prescriptions during your visit or you can pick them up at a later time.

Our Pharmacy Offers:

  • Prescription medications
  • Flea and tick preventatives
  • Heartworm preventatives
  • Prescription diets
  • Supplements

Online Pharmacy

For your convenience, we also offer an online pharmacy where you can order medications and have them shipped directly to your home.
