Sunnymead Animal Hospital Achieves AAHA Accreditation!

We are happy to announce that our hospital has received accreditation from the American Animal Hospital Association. AAHA accreditation means that we have met the stringent standards set out by AAHA and you can rest assured that your pet will receive the highest standard of care. Meet Our Team

Dr. Ruben Ontiveros - Dr. Ontiveros attended California State University, Pomona, for his undergraduate degree. He then went on to obtain his DVM from the University of California, Davis. After graduation, he did an internship in small animal medicine & surgery at VCA West Los Angeles. Shortly after, he returned to his home town of Moreno Valley where he became the owner of Sunnymead Animal Hospital in 2002. He strives to give his patients an excellent standard of care in a family-like atmosphere.

Dr. Sarah Lauro - Dr. Lauro is a Southern California native. She began her college career at California State Polytechnic University, Pomona where she majored in Animal Health Science and worked at the W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center. She went on to obtain her DVM from Western University of Health Sciences. Dr. Lauro's professional interests include internal medicine, theriogenology, and soft tissue surgery.
